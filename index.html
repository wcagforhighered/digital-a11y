Federal digital accessibility compliance deadlines

ADA Title II: April 2027
HHS Section 504: May 11, 2027
Required Standard: WCAG 2.1 Level AA

Universities without a systematic audit and remediation plan face mounting legal, financial, and reputational risk. Proactive auditing of high-impact content—PDFs, data science notebooks, and slide decks—now positions your institution to meet these deadlines without crisis-mode scrambling.

Data & Security

We understand institutional data requirements. Here's how we handle content:

  • Sample data only: Engagements use sample or de-identified content — no PII, no FERPA-protected student records.
  • No retained storage: Reports and remediated files are delivered and removed from our systems upon completion.
  • FERPA-aware workflow: Our process is designed to avoid handling protected student information entirely.

We're happy to review specific data handling requirements with your security or procurement office before engagement.
